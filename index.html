Steadirect.com

URL Protocol Converter

Convert protocol URLs (like Steam invites) to shareable HTTPS links that work on any platform.

URL Converter Form

How to use:

  1. Enter your Protocol URL in the format: steam://joinlobby/123456/987654321
  2. Click "Convert" to generate the shareable link
  3. The generated link will always be in this format: https://steadirect.com/?url=steam://joinlobby/123456/987654321
  4. Use the copy button to copy the link

This is an independent tool and is not endorsed by or affiliated with Valve Corporation or other platforms.
All URL conversion and redirection is performed locally in your browser - no data is collected or logged.
